Privacy Policy

Your photos stay yours. Always.

Last updated: January 2026

MemeScanr is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal photos, videos, and data should remain fully under your control.

What Data MemeScanr Accesses

  • Photo and video access is used only to scan your local gallery
  • All scanning and analysis happens on your device
  • No images or videos are uploaded to external servers

What Data MemeScanr Does NOT Collect

  • No personal identity information
  • No location data
  • No contacts, messages, or browsing history
  • No advertising or tracking identifiers

On-Device Processing

MemeScanr uses on-device intelligence to identify memes, duplicates, and clutter. This ensures faster performance and maximum privacy. Your content never leaves your phone.

Email & Waitlist
