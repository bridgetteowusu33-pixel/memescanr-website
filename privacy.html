Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

MemeScanr does not collect or store your personal photos, memes, or contacts. All scanning and cleanup happens locally on your device.

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not include personal information.

If you have questions or concerns, contact us at support@memescanr.com.
